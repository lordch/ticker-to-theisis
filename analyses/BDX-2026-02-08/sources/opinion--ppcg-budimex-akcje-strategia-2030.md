# Budimex akcje: nowa strategia 2030, dywidenda i boom inwestycyjny - PPCG Stock

## Strategy 2030 Overview
Budimex management plans a decade-long strategic framework focused on accelerating growth from historical 6-7% annually while maintaining margin discipline. CEO Artur Popko stated: "patrzac na skale inwestycji chcemy przyspieszyc ten proces, przy utrzymaniu rentownosci."

## Five Strategic Pillars
1. **Core Construction**: Roads, railways, airport, and civil engineering
2. **International Expansion**: Baltic states, Slovakia, Czech Republic
3. **Services Segment**: Long-term maintenance contracts (FBSerwis)
4. **Renewable Energy**: Building owned generating asset portfolio
5. **Developer Model**: Residential projects in partnerships

## Dividend Policy
- **2025-2030 Commitment**: 100% net profit distribution
- **2024 Dividend**: 25.43 PLN/share (4.38% yield)
- **Historical Average**: 4-10% yields, with peaks at 13.54% (2011)

## Investment Thesis - Growth Drivers

**Market Size**: Management estimates Polish investment market through 2040 at **1.5 trillion PLN**

**2026 Tender Pipeline**:
- Port Polska (CPK): 24.8B PLN
- GDDKiA road projects: 12B PLN
- PKP PLK railways: 9.5B PLN
- Energy networks: 4.5B PLN

## New Sector Entries

**Military/Defense**: MON spending surged from 1.6B PLN (2019) to planned 10B PLN (2025); Budimex holds NATO Secret certifications

**Nuclear & Renewables**: Currently operating 87 MW renewable capacity; targeting 500 MW within two years; positioning for Polish nuclear projects

**Data Centers**: Expanding beyond construction into operator/owner roles via hyperscaler partnerships
