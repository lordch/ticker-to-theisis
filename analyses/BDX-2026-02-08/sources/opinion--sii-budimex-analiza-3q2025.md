# Budimex - budowanie skali na nowych rynkach (SII analiza 3Q 2025)

## Financial Position & Backlog
- **Backlog (end Q3 2025):** Declined 1.6 billion PLN during 9-month period
- **Order portfolio history:** ~13 billion PLN (2021-2023) -> 17.8 billion PLN (2024)
- **Expected year-end backlog:** Management forecasts exceeding 18 billion PLN
- **Contracts pending signature:** 5.1 billion PLN (including 2.5 billion in rail segment)
- **Outstanding bids:** Over 10.5 billion PLN in value, with Rail Baltica (Latvia/Estonia) representing 5 billion PLN

## Geographic/Segment Expansion
**Energy Transition:** Won 134 million PLN contract for 110/220 kV electro-energy station near Zary (September 2025). Expanding Polish Energy Network segment organically to capture multi-billion PLN modernization program through 2036.

**Rail:** Consortium with Gulermak won 1.69 billion PLN contract for railway line 622 Szczyrzyc-Tymbark. Rail Baltica bids pending major decision.

## Market Environment
The analysis notes construction-assembly market contraction since 2024, with "normalization of margins after strong 2021-2023 period." Commodity prices stabilized (steel, concrete, asphalt). Newer EU procurement rules limiting non-EU competitors improving competitive dynamics.

## Investment Thesis
Company positioned as infrastructure beneficiary: government road spending projected 34-38 billion PLN (2027-2029), then 77 billion PLN (2031-2033). Work secured through 2027.
