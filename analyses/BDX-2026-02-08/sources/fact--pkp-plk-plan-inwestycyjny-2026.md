# PKP PLK plany inwestycyjne na 2026 rok

## 2026 Total Tender Value
Approximately **9.5 billion PLN** planned for public procurement announcements in 2026, with updates expected in Q1 2026.

## 2025 Summary
- Original plan: 16 billion PLN
- Updated plan: ~27 billion PLN in total tender announcements by end of 2025
- Already announced: ~26 billion PLN

## 2026 Project Breakdown by Quarter

**Q1 2026** (7 major projects):
- Gdow-Szczyrzyc rail section (design and construction)
- Szczecin main line improvements
- Torun railway line modernization (line 353) with BiT City II
- Line 24 modernization and electrification (Piotrkow-Belchatow-Bogumilowa)
- Line 16 modernization (Leczyca-Kutno)
- Wroclaw section reconstruction

**Q2 2026** (3 major projects):
- Tychy-Bierun Stary reconstruction
- Lines 140 and 169 work (Orzesze Jaskowice-Tychy)
- Krakow-Olkusz rail connection

**Q3 2026** (3 major projects):
- Multiple line work (4, 64, 570)
- Modlin airport connection to Warsaw
- Lines 189 and 132 work with new junctions

**Q4 2026** (3 major projects):
- Czarnkow-Rogozno-Wagrowiec revitalization
- Krakow-Niepolomice connection
- Wroclaw metropolitan railway improvements
