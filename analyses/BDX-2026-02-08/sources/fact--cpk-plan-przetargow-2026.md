# Plan postepowan inwestycyjnych CPK - przetargi na ponad 40 mld zl w 2026

## Total Tender Value
- **2026 projected value**: ~40 billion PLN
- **2025 projected value**: ~30 billion PLN (18 billion PLN already announced by end-August)

## 2026 Quarterly Breakdown
- Q1: ~7 billion PLN
- Q2: ~20 billion PLN
- Q3: ~3 billion PLN
- Q4: ~10 billion PLN

## By Investment Type

**Rail (KDP - High-Speed Rail)**
- Warsaw-Lodz line construction works (primary Q2 2026 tender)
- Railway station with platform hall (2026)
- Two initial sections: Warsaw Zachodnia-Konotopy (18 km); Konotopy-CPK with Parzniew-Kotowice connector (13 km)
- Framework agreement value: >10 billion PLN (covering earthworks, terminal foundations, tunnel under airport)

**Roads**
- >2.2 billion PLN for airport perimeter roads
- >90 km of roads to be built/rebuilt
- Two separate framework agreements with Polish-capital entities included

**Airport/Landside**
- Terminal building construction framework agreement (planned 2026)
- Navigational lighting system (2026)
- Landside East/West transport systems (2026)
- Fuel base and Railway Front Unloading Facility (planned 2027, 1-3 billion PLN)

## Strategic Commentary
Authority emphasizes "preferential treatment for Polish language proficiency among key personnel, knowledge of Polish law, and experience with investments in Poland" to direct funds domestically and create employment.

**Project deadline**: New airport operational by 2032; KDP first segment (Warsaw-Lodz) also 2032.
