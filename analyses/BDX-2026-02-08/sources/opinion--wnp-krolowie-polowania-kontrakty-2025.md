# Oto "krolowie polowania" - kto zgarnal najwiecej na rynku budowlanym 2025 - WNP

## Road Contracts (GDDKiA)
**Leader: Mirbud** - 7 contracts worth 1.533 billion PLN
- Budimex: 5 contracts, 1.408 billion PLN (#2 position)
- Strabag: 2 contracts, 874.43 million PLN (largest single deal: 798.2 million PLN for Glogow bypass)
- Intercor: 2 contracts, 323.46 million PLN
- Mostostal Warszawa: 1 contract, 305.79 million PLN

**Total sector value:** 23 contracts, 5.1+ billion PLN

## Rail Projects (PKP PLK)
**Leader: PORR** - 7 contracts, 2.181 billion PLN
- Torpol: 3 contracts, 1.950 billion PLN
- Budimex (with Gulermak): 4 contracts, 1.770 billion PLN (#3 position)
- Trakcja: 4 contracts, 1.708 billion PLN
- PNUI Krakow: 4 contracts, 1.300 billion PLN

## Central Airport Project (CPK)
**Largest single contract:** PORR - 2.1 billion PLN for high-speed rail tunnel under Lodz
**Additional winners:**
- Trakcja: 2 contracts (transport corridors): 147.5-206.52 million PLN + 110.5 million PLN
- Unibep/Track Tec: 143 million PLN (rail siding)

## Energy Networks (PSE)
**Leader: Enprom** - 4 contracts, 460.8 million PLN
- SPIE Energy Poland: 3 contracts, 297.66 million PLN
- Agat: 1 contract, 288.42 million PLN
- Budimex: 1 contract, 134.1 million PLN

## Regional Airport Contracts
**Leader: Strabag** - 114 million PLN (Rzeszow-Jasionka runway)

**Key insight:** Rail investments dominated 2025, with PKP PLK alone signing four contracts exceeding 1 billion PLN each.
