# Poland Construction Industry Report 2025 - GlobalData/ResearchAndMarkets

## Performance Metrics
- **2025 Contraction**: 0.2% shrinkage in real terms
- **2026-2029 Growth**: 3.9% average annual growth rate (AAGR)

## 2025 Headwinds
- Building permits fell 4.9% year-over-year in first nine months
- Construction and assembly costs grew 3.2% YoY
- Labor costs increased 7.1% YoY; prior year was 13.8%
- Budget deficit reached PLN 201.4 billion ($49.8B) by September

## Growth Drivers (2026-2029)
Three primary sectors will fuel recovery:

1. **Renewable Energy**: Government targets 51.8% electricity from renewables by 2030 (up from 29% in 2024); nearly 80% by 2040
2. **Nuclear Power**: Part of National Energy and Climate Plan
3. **Transport Infrastructure**: PLN 290 billion ($71.7B) allocated under National Road Construction Program for 2,500km of motorways/expressways by 2033

## Additional Investment
- PLN 18 billion ($4.5B) for healthcare facility development via National Reconstruction Plan

## Sector Coverage
The report segments analysis across: commercial, industrial, infrastructure, energy/utilities, institutional, and residential construction.
