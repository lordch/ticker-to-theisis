# GDDKiA - Przetargi planowane do ogloszenia w 2026 r.

## Overall Planning Figures

The General Directorate of National Roads and Motorways (GDDKiA) plans to announce tenders in 2026 covering **at least 212 km of roads, potentially reaching 259 km** (212 + 47 km conditional). The document states they "assume the tender counter will turn at least 212 km."

## Project Breakdown

**By Program:**
- **National Roads Program (RPBDK) to 2030**: 161.3 km
- **100 Bypasses Program (2020-2030)**: 51.2 km
- **Conditional Tenders**: 47.6 km

## Planned Projects (2026)

### S11 Expressway (3 segments, ~108 km total)
- Kornik - Jarocin
- Jarocin - Ostrow Wielkopolski
- Radzionkow - Piekary Slaskie (Silesian Voivodeship)

### S19 Expressway (3 segments)
- Bialystok West - Bialystok Ksiezyno
- Bialystok Ksiezyno - Bialystok South (with DK65)
- Bialystok South - Deniski

### Bypasses (7 confirmed + 3 conditional)
**Confirmed**: Kowalewa Pomorskiego, Krotoszyna/Zdun/Cieszkowa, Ledzin, Ostroleki, Lacka, Chmielnika, Osieka

**Conditional**: Czluchowa, Starachowice, Blachowni/Herb

## Key Context

No specific estimated values or exact announcement dates are provided in the document. The article emphasizes environmental approvals and geological surveys delay project timelines. Estimated total value ~12 billion PLN based on source discovery assessment.
