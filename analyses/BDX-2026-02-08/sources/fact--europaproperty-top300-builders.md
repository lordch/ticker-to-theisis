# Top 300 Polish Builders Earn PLN 150 billion in 2024 - EuropaProperty

## Total Market Revenue
**PLN 150 billion** in combined turnover for Poland's 300 largest construction companies in 2024. This represented approximately **60% of revenues** from medium and large construction firms sector-wide.

## Market Concentration & Leadership
The sector exhibits **high fragmentation** with slow consolidation. Market leader Budimex Group held **3.9% market share** (2023 data). The top five builders -- Budimex, Strabag, Porr, Mirbud, and Erbud -- commanded **11.3% of revenue** from companies employing 9+ staff.

## Revenue Thresholds for Market Entry
- **Top 300 entry**: PLN 133 million minimum (2023)
- **Top 100 entry**: PLN 360 million minimum
- **Top 50 entry**: PLN 675 million minimum

## Segment Leaders
**Civil engineering**: Budimex, Polimex-Mostostal, Mirbud, Tecnicas Reunidas, Strabag
**Non-residential**: Budimex, Adamietz, Warbud, Porr, Strabag
**Residential**: Unibep, Erbud, Budimex, Skanska

## Market Dynamics
Despite 2024 downturn conditions, "positive inflation in the construction industry allowed the largest players to maintain a similar level of revenues as in 2023." Order books showed **double-digit growth rates** by year-end 2024, driven by EU fund releases and continued mega-project investment.
