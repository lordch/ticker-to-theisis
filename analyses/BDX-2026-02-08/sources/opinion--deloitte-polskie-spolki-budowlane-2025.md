## Profitability Metrics

Operating margin for the 15 largest firms declined by 350 million PLN (-0.2% annually). Key performers:

- **Budimex Group**: 1.2 billion PLN in operating profit (highest)
- **VINCI Construction Polska – Warbud, Eurovia**: 308 million PLN
- **Unibep Group**: Largest nominal improvement (+214 million PLN improvement from prior year's negative result)

## Market Size & Growth

Polish construction market valued at **353.5 billion PLN** (4.3% growth in current prices). Building construction sector led expansion with **20.1% YoY production increase**.

## Key Industry Trends

Per survey data: "Zrównoważone budownictwo i ekologiczne technologie" (sustainable construction and green technologies) identified as most influential trend for market development over next 5 years.

## Strategic Challenges

- High interest rates deterring new project investment
- Limited supply of cubic construction projects (residential, office, warehouse)
- Labor shortages
- Rising operational costs
- Innovation expense pressures

## Growth Drivers

Major infrastructure projects: energy sector investments, CPK (Central Polish Airport), highway and railway construction networks will stabilize contractor backlogs.
