# Popko (Budimex): Energetyka kluczowa w strategii rozwoju - Rynek Infrastruktury

**Publication Date:** June 16, 2025

## Key Financial Figures

**PSE Investment Plan:**
- Polish Power Grid (PSE) planning investments at approximately 64 billion zlotys over upcoming years
- Budimex submitted three competitive bids for high-voltage transmission lines totaling nearly 180 kilometers

## Strategic Goals

**CEO Artur Popko Statement:**
"We want to engage more strongly in developing atomic energy and electrical grid networks."

**5-10 Year Vision:**
- Achieve leadership position in Central-Eastern European infrastructure market
- Focus on largest strategic infrastructure contracts
- Expand beyond domestic Polish markets

## Nuclear Energy Program

- Government estimates: 8,000 workers for first nuclear plant construction
- Over 50,000 workers needed across entire Polish Nuclear Energy Program
- Described as economy-transforming initiative affecting whole industrial sector
- Expected outcome: Future electricity price reductions benefiting material costs and GDP growth

## Strategic Areas

1. **Atomic energy development**
2. **Electrical grid modernization**
3. **High-voltage transmission infrastructure**
4. **Renewable energy support infrastructure**

## Workforce & Industrial Development

Program aims to develop complete new industrial sector with scientific-technical infrastructure, utilizing Polish contractors and local business capabilities.
