# Segmenty operacyjne Grupy Budimex - raport roczny 2022

## Identified Operating Segments

1. **Infrastructure Construction** - road, bridge, railway, aviation, hydrotechnical projects
2. **Railway Construction** - rail infrastructure and specialized equipment services
3. **General Construction** - public utility, commercial, industrial, residential, sports facilities
4. **Energy and Industrial Construction** - power plant and industrial projects
5. **Renewable Energy Sources (RES)** - wind and solar farm development
6. **Laboratory Services** - quality control and technological testing
7. **Equipment Services** - earthworks, crane services, transport
8. **Mineral and Asphalt Mixture** - recycled road materials production

## Supporting Companies
- Mostostal Krakow SA (steel structures)
- FBSerwis SA (facility management, road maintenance)
- Budimex Kolejnictwo SA (railway equipment services)
- Circular Construction SA (waste recycling)
- Budimex Mobility SA (EV charging stations)

## Note
Page contains operational descriptions only; no financial data by segment. From source discovery: infrastructure grew to 40.2% of revenues in 2023 vs 38.8% in 2022.
