# Budimex w IV kwartale poprawil zysk netto o 80 proc. r/r - StockWatch

## Q4 2025 Financial Results

**Q4 2025 Performance:**
- Net profit: 310 million PLN (+80% year-over-year)
- Revenue: 3.0 billion PLN
- Operating profit (EBIT): 383 million PLN
- EBIT margin: 12.8% (vs. 9.1% in Q4 2024)
- Net margin: 10.3% (vs. 6.6% in Q4 2024)

**Comparative Q4 2024:**
- Revenue: 2.607 billion PLN
- EBIT: 236 million PLN
- Net profit: 172 million PLN

## Order Portfolio
- Q4 2025 new contracts: 2.4 billion PLN
- Backlog (end of December 2025): 16.2 billion PLN

## Segment Performance
Strong results driven by improved profitability in the general construction segment and high profitability in the service segment.

## Analyst Commentary
Dariusz Nawrot (Noble Securities) highlighted that Q4 delivered "record financial results" with gross margins in construction reaching approximately 17.5% -- the highest in nine years -- attributable to "resolving reserves on contracts in final execution stages."

## Stock Performance
Share price trading near 705 PLN (up over 5% on announcement day).
